Who We Are

We are a team of students who understand the struggle of buying costly equipment for short-term use. Our mission is to provide affordable, eco-friendly rentals for engineering essentials.

Our vision is to make engineering education more accessible by reducing the financial burden of purchasing tools and equipment. We believe in sustainability and promoting a sharing economy, so every item is reused responsibly, saving both money and resources.

With a simple 3-step process – browse, rent, return – students can focus on learning and projects without worrying about equipment costs. We prioritize convenience, reliability, and safety in all our rental services.

Join our community and be part of a smarter, greener, and more affordable way to access engineering tools and resources.
