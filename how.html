How It Works

1. Browse Items

Explore our catalog of engineering essentials such as drafters, lab aprons, calculators, and more.

2. Select & Pay

Choose the items you need, select the rental period, and pay a refundable deposit online.

3. Collect or Delivery

Pick up your items from our center or get them delivered directly to your college or home.

4. Return & Refund

Return the rented items in good condition at the end of the rental period to get your deposit back.
